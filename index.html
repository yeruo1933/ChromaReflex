Welcome to ChromaReflex

A precision tool for training your visual reaction speed — built around colour perception science.

1

Watch the circle

A large colour circle appears on screen. Stare at it. At a random moment between 2 and 8 seconds, it will begin slowly shifting to a completely different hue.

2

Click the instant you see it change

The moment your eye detects the colour shift, click anywhere on the circle. The transition is gradual — not a sudden flash. You're training yourself to catch the very first hint of change.

3

Don't click early — or the rep restarts

Clicking before the colour starts changing triggers a false start. The rep resets and you try again. Anticipating the change is not allowed — only reacting to it.

4

Your time is perceptually adjusted

The timer starts the moment the colour begins to change — but the first few frames are below the human eye's detection threshold (ΔE < 2.3 JND). That invisible portion is automatically subtracted, so your score reflects true neural + motor reaction time, not physics lag.

5

5 reps, then your average

Each session is 5 rounds. At the end you'll see your adjusted average, each rep's raw vs. adjusted time, and how you rank on the leaderboard.

Three difficulty levels

Difficulty controls how fast the colour fades and how close the two colours are — making the change harder to spot.

Easy — 2s fade · 90° gap Medium — 3s fade · 70° gap Hard — 5s fade · 60° gap

Each difficulty has its own leaderboard. Scores are not cross-compared between difficulties.
